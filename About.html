I am a Non-Binary Singaporean/Metis Film Editor born in Vancouver, British Columbia that is currently based in Toronto, Ontario. While attending a specialized acting program in highschool, I found myself enjoying and frequently editing friends and fellow classmate’s video assignments.
After highschool, I went to pursue an editing career studying at Fanshawe College for Interactive Media Design and Production. Over the 3 years of studying, I learned many other skills such as Graphic Design, Photography, Motion Design, and basic web coding (HTML/CSS). Following that, I had obtained a Certificate for Digital Media Production at Humber College.
I am interested in all forms of creative visual storytelling, and am always seeking new ways to express a message.
